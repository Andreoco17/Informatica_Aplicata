Plot

When his father died, Denji was stuck with a huge debt and no way to pay it back. Thanks to a Devil dog he saved named Pochita, he's able to survive through odd jobs and killing Devils for the Yakuza. Pochita's chainsaw powers come in handy against these powerful demons. When the Yakuza betrays him and he's killed by the Zombie Devil, Pochita sacrifices himself to save his former master. Denji is then reborn as a Devil-Human hybrid known as Chainsaw Man.

After his transformation, he's quickly recruited by Makima to join the Public Safety Devil Hunters under threat of extermination now that he's technically a Devil. Now that he's living comfortably for the first time in his life, Denji struggles to determine his dreams and form meaningful relationships while killing Devils and working alongside other eccentric Devil Hunters.

1st Popularity Poll

The first popularity poll took place between February and May 2020, allowing users to vote once a day for their favorite character. Results were announced in the 26th 2020 issue of Shonen Jump. The total number of votes was 231,201. Results were presented in a color spread depicting the winners at a beach wearing traditional Japanese school uniforms, which differ from the canonical ones worn by Asa Mitaka and others in Part 2. This spread was later released as a jigsaw puzzle from Ensky.

Tatsuki Fujimoto





Tatsuki Fujimoto


Biography

Tatsuki often drew images at an early age. In Junior High School he thought of a magazine in his head and often cut and serialized new works in it, usually serializing seven works. In his first years of high school, he started to upload manga to the website Shintosha.


After graduating, he made several one shots participating in Manga Awards Contents and had won some of them and got noticed by ONE and Sui Ishida. Later those oneshots were republished on Jump+ or Jump SQ.19 magazine. Fujimoto won Honorable Mentions in the November 2013 Shueisha Crown Newcomers' Awards for his debut one-shot story "Love is Blind". He made his official debut in Jump SQ.19 with "Shikaku" and following year published "Yogen no Nayuta" in Jump SQ.


Tatsuki made his first serialization with Fire Punch in 2016, ran for eight volumes, published on Jump+ site. The series was proposed for a Jump SQ magazine, but as it was declined, the editor proposed it for a Jump+ serialization and it was approved. The series ran for 8 volumes and gathered the internet attention due to its shocking developments.


After Fire Punch serialization ended, Tatsuki published a new oneshot in Jump SQ 2018-06 Issue, titled Imouto no An


In December 2018, he started a new serialization Chainsaw Man in Weekly Shonen Jump.


He is active on Twitter as @nagayama_koharu, where he tweets in character as an imaginary younger sister in third grade.


During November 2022, Tatsuki lost access to his primary account nagayama_koharu and created a new temporary one under the user handle @ashitaka_eva, at first fans were skeptical of his identity until Fujimoto's editor Shihei Lin confirmed his identity to fans through this tweet and Tatsuki shared some prototype designs for Aki, Power, and Denji's hybrid form on his new account. After Tatsuki was able to recover his main Twitter account, the temporary one he created was made inactive.
